Prepare the Cookies Dough
Mix the dry ingredients (flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, salt) in one bowl. In another bowl, cream the butter and sugars, then add eggs and vanilla. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
Prepare the Cookies Base
Shape the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Add Chocolate
Press chocolate chips into the top of each cookie dough ball.
Bake the Cookies
Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes. Let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
Ingredients
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
3/4 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 large eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups chocolate chips
