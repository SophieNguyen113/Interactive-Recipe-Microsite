Make the Pizza Dough
In a large bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms, then knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
Let the Dough Rise
Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-1.5 hours until doubled in size.
Shape and Add Sauce
Punch down the dough and roll it out into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a pizza pan or baking sheet. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border for the crust.
Add Cheese
Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce, covering the entire surface.
Add Pepperoni
Arrange pepperoni slices evenly over the cheese. You can also add any other toppings you like at this stage.
Bake the Pizza
Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Let it cool for 5 minutes before slicing.
Ingredients
1 cup warm water (110°F)
1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
1 teaspoon sugar
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup pizza sauce
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
20-25 pepperoni slices
Optional: Italian seasoning, garlic powder
