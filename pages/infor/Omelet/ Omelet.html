Crack & Beat Eggs
Crack 3 eggs into a bowl. Season with salt and pepper. This is the foundation of your perfect omelet, fresh eggs make all the difference.
Whisk the Mixture
Beat the eggs vigorously with a fork or whisk until the yolks and whites are completely combined. Add a splash of milk or cream for extra fluffiness if desired.
ü•Ñ
Cook in Pan
Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour in the egg mixture and let it cook undisturbed for 30 seconds, then gently stir with a spatula, tilting the pan to let uncooked egg flow to the edges.
Fold & Serve
When the eggs are almost set but still slightly wet on top, add your fillings to one half. Fold the omelet in half, slide onto a plate, and serve immediately while hot.
Ingredients
3 large eggs
2 tablespoons milk or cream
2 tablespoons butter
Salt to taste
Black pepper to taste
Fresh herbs for garnish
